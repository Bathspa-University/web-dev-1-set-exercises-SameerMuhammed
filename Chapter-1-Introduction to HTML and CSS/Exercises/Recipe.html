PANCAKE RECIPE

Perfect pancakes are easier to make than you think. This pancake recipe produces thick, fluffy, and all-around delicious pancakes with just a few ingredients that are probably already in your kitchen (and it's so much better than the boxed stuff).

INGREDIENTS

  • 3tbsp melted butter
  • 1 1/4 c milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 1 1/2 c flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder

METHOD

  1. Melt butter in microwave
  2. Warm milk in microwave for approx. 1 min
  3. Whisk butter and milk
  4. Add egg and whisk
  5. Add vanilla and sugar and whisk
  6. Add remaining ingredients and whisk together (may be a little lumpy)
  7. Pour approx. Â¼ c batter onto griddle to form pancake
  8. Flip when you see bubbles forming in batter
  9. Check underside for golden brown to know when pancake is finished
